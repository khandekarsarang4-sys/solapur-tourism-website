Welcome to Solapur

Solapur is a historic and culturally rich city located in Maharashtra near the Karnataka and Telangana borders.

The city is famous for spiritual destinations such as Siddheshwar Temple, Pandharpur and Akkalkot.

Solapur is also known for its textile products like Solapur chaddars and cotton towels.

About Solapur

Solapur is a historic and culturally rich city in Maharashtra near Karnataka and Telangana.

It is famous for spiritual centers like Siddheshwar Temple, Pandharpur and Akkalkot.

The city is also known for traditional textiles such as Solapur chaddars and towels.
